CRUMPLED BALL - 1

Hello everyone! The following are the rules to play this game :-

1. In this project you have to press the arrow key to make the crumpled paper ball go inside the dustbin
2. The crumpled ball is set at a random velocity. So, to play again you will need to refresh the page.
3. I have also added a Matter Renderer so that you can see the Matter Bodies.

Enjoy The Game!
